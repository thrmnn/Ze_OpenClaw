## Reporting to Mission Control
At the START of your work, run:
  bash ~/clawd/scripts/mc-agent-report.sh start "AGENT_NAME" "brief task description"

At each major milestone, run:
  bash ~/clawd/scripts/mc-agent-report.sh progress "AGENT_NAME" "what you just completed"

When COMPLETELY DONE, run:
  bash ~/clawd/scripts/mc-agent-report.sh done "AGENT_NAME" "one-line summary of all outcomes"

If you hit an error you cannot recover from:
  bash ~/clawd/scripts/mc-agent-report.sh error "AGENT_NAME" "what failed and why"
